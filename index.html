Switzerland's
Jewelry Landscape

📍 Strategic advantage: Lucerne sits at the crossroads of Switzerland's two main jewelry hubs — Geneva (west, 2h) and Zürich (north, 45min). Both Gübelin and Bucherer — two of Switzerland's most prestigious jewelers — are headquartered in Lucerne itself. This proximity enables fast delivery, confidential production, and face-to-face client relationships.

Expansion Markets

Europe's Key
Jewelry Hubs

🇩🇪

Germany

Pforzheim — "The Golden City"

Europe's highest concentration of jewelry manufacturers per capita. Hundreds of mid-size producers that routinely outsource stone setting for fine and high-end collections. Strong tradition of B2B subcontracting.

⏱ ~2.5h from Lucerne
🇮🇹

Italy

Valenza · Vicenza · Arezzo

The "Italian Gold" triangle — the world capital of high jewelry manufacturing. Valenza is the epicenter of fine jewelry design. These ateliers require specialist setters for premium gem-set collections destined for global luxury markets.

⏱ ~3–4h from Lucerne
🇫🇷

France

Paris — Place Vendôme

Home to the world's most prestigious jewelry houses — Van Cleef & Arpels, Cartier, Boucheron, Chanel Fine Jewelry. While largely vertically integrated, they outsource specialist setting during peak collection development periods.

⏱ ~4h from Lucerne
🇬🇧

United Kingdom

London — Hatton Garden

The most significant fine jewelry district in Northern Europe. Hundreds of independent luxury jewelers actively look for trusted European setting partners — post-Brexit, Swiss suppliers are highly valued for quality assurance.

⏱ ~2h flight from Zürich
04
Go-To-Market

How to Reach
These Clients

1

Start in Lucerne — Immediate Wins

Bucherer and Gübelin are headquartered in the same city. Request a face-to-face meeting with their Head of Production or Atelier Director. Present a portfolio, offer an NDA, and propose a small pilot project. These two relationships alone could anchor the business.

In-Person Visit Portfolio Presentation NDA Offer Pilot Project
2

LinkedIn B2B Direct Outreach

Search for "Production Manager", "Head of Atelier", "Sourcing Manager" or "Jewelry Manufacturing Director" at Swiss and European jewelry houses. Send a short, direct message with a portfolio PDF attached. Focus on your Swiss location as a quality signal and your specialty in specific setting techniques (pavé, invisible set, bezel, etc.).

LinkedIn Sales Navigator Cold Outreach PDF Portfolio
3

Trade Fairs — Where Buyers Come to You

The most effective channel for B2B jewelry services. Exhibiting at Inhorgenta (Munich) or Baselworld places you in front of 500+ jewelry buyers and production managers in 3 days. Even attending as a visitor with cards and portfolio is valuable. See Section 05 for the full fair calendar.

Inhorgenta Munich Baselworld VicenzaOro Italy
4

Industry Associations — Credibility & Network

Join the Swiss Jewellery and Watch Industry Federation (FHS) and CIBJO (World Jewellery Confederation). Membership opens referral networks, event access, and buyer introductions. The RJC (Responsible Jewellery Council) certification is highly valued by luxury brands and should be obtained early.

FHS Switzerland CIBJO RJC Certification
5

The Core Sales Message

Every touchpoint should reinforce one clear proposition: expert stone setting, Swiss precision, fast delivery, full confidentiality. Luxury brands pay a premium for Swiss-made components — your location is a competitive advantage, not just a detail.

Swiss Precision Confidential NDA Fast Turnaround Specialized Techniques
